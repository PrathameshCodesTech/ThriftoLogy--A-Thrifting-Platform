Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping second-hand fashion with Thriftology

Product Availability

As a thrift store, each piece in our collection is unique and one-of-a-kind. We source pre-loved items, which means we typically only have one size available for each style. This makes our pieces exclusive and special!

We update our inventory daily with new finds! Since each item is unique, we recommend checking back frequently or following our social media for updates on new arrivals.

Due to the unique nature of thrifted fashion, exact items usually can't be restocked. However, you can sign up for notifications to be alerted when similar items in your size become available.

Product Quality

Each item goes through a rigorous quality check process. We examine for any damage, verify authenticity, clean and sanitize items, and make minor repairs if needed. Only pieces that meet our quality standards are listed.

We use a detailed condition rating system:

  • Like New: Shows no signs of wear
  • Excellent: Minor signs of wear, no visible flaws
  • Very Good: Light wear, minor fixable flaws noted in description
  • Good: Normal wear, any flaws clearly documented

Shopping & Returns

Yes! We offer a 14-day return policy for store credit. Due to the unique nature of our items, we encourage reading size measurements carefully before purchasing.

Our pricing reflects the item's original retail value, current condition, brand, rarity, and vintage status. We aim to provide fair prices that make sustainable fashion accessible while supporting our mission.

Sustainability

Each thrifted item purchased reduces demand for new production, saving water and reducing carbon emissions. By choosing pre-loved clothing, you're helping extend the lifecycle of garments and reducing textile waste.

We use eco-friendly cleaning products, recyclable packaging, and minimize waste in our operations. We also partner with textile recycling programs for items we can't sell.
